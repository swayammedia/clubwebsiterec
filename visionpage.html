OUR VISION




The vision of this club is to create a vibrant community of coding and tech enthusiasts based out of REC! We want to work on useful projects that helps the individual and the community grow and benefit mutually





To make this vision a reality we require passionate and driven members who are reciprocative to the work that is being put in and contribute meaningfully! Let's go code now
